# Week 7 - AI Prompt Log

## Prompt 1

### Prompt
Write a product description for the following product.

Product Name: Wooden Vase
Material: Handmade Wood
Category: Home Decor

### Example Output
A simple handcrafted wooden vase made for home decoration.

### Result
The description was too short and lacked detail.

Enhance your living space with this handcrafted wooden vase made from premium-quality wood. Designed by skilled artisans, it combines durability with elegance...

### Result
The description was better, but sometimes varied in length.

---

## Prompt 3 (Final Prompt)

### Prompt
You are an expert e-commerce copywriter.

Write a professional product description.

Product Name: Wooden Vase
Material: Handmade Wood
Category: Home Decor

Requirements:
- Around 100 words
- Attractive
- SEO friendly
- Mention craftsmanship
- End with a call to action.

### Example Output
Bring timeless elegance to your home with our handcrafted wooden vase. Carefully crafted by skilled artisans using premium-quality wood, this decorative piece blends traditional craftsmanship with modern style. Its durable design and natural finish make it perfect for any living space. Add warmth and character to your home while supporting local artisans. Order yours today and experience authentic handmade craftsmanship.

### Result
This prompt consistently produced professional, well-structured, and SEO-friendly descriptions.

---

# Best Prompt

Prompt 3 worked best because it clearly specified the desired length, writing style, SEO requirements, craftsmanship, and call to action. These instructions helped the AI generate consistent, high-quality product descriptions suitable for the KalaSetu marketplace.
